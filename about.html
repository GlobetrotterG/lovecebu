About Love Cebu!

Who we Are

Love Cebu! was founded by Geoffrey C. Hutchinson on the 1st of June 2020. He is a Cebu-born based in London, UK who is a Blogger, Travel enthusiast, Student of Life, and a Software Developer.

Along with his team, who are based in Cebu, Philippines, Love Cebu! Was created for travel enthusiasts who are curious to discover and visit the island of Cebu.

They aim to share the beauty and the wonders of what this island has to offer.

We hope that this site has given you a choice for your next travel destination, or better yet, include it in your bucket list!
